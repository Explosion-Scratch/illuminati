Per Aspera Ad Astra

 

Eternal chaos and a perpetual alteration of time bring forth moments of awakening. And the infinite space allows wonders of which a human mind can't construct an imagination.

Our Cosmos could be seen through various sights, could be felt through various senses, it could be observed from many different angles; we will take a look at it from the human view...

Your little life with its short limited duration, is not even a single flare up in the helix of being. Your daily endeavors and so important duties, feign your mind's center to simulate you a reason, an importance for being. This fake serves your surviving as it behaves with many other deceptions of the nature. This illusion gives you your little required sense of life and the satisfaction that you are here, alive, and that your importance of being serves other benefits. But often you may ask yourself: Was that all? Is this all that life offers? Am I not ordained for more than just this?

The diverseness of the human nature can't offer a unique solution and it requires therefore variations to recognize one's individual direction.

Our direction points straight to the center of the unknown and inexpressible, our path begins where all others end, and our minds awake in spheres whose gates others never could open.

To let you taste a piece of it, we invite you to participate in our program. Your attendance is absolutely free of any charges but it requires a priori a test of your mind, your body and your soul.

As a result a path will be open for the proper candidate.
