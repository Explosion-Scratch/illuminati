Occult Symbols

 

The following listing was taken from the magazine Satanism In America: What They Do Not Want You To Know, pages 41and 42.
Pentagram: Symbolizes the Morning Star, a name that Satan has taken. Tau Cross: This cross was inscribed on the forehead of every person admitted into the Mysteries of Mithras. Mithras was a god of the ancient Persians and the Aryans of India. This symbol is preserved in modern masonry under the symbol of the T square.
Hexagram: Also known as the Star of David or Crest of Solomon. This symbol was used by the Egyptians long before the Israelis adopted it. Evil Spirits gather in force at the summons of the person working the "Hex". Italian Horn, Unicorn Horn or Leprechaun Staff: Was introduced by the Lord Druids of Scotland and Ireland. It was used as the instrument of the Druids for castrating animals and humans. It has been hexed and is used for luck and fortune.
Ankh: Symbolizes the fertility rites and the building up of lust within a person. Triangle or Pyramid and Evil Eye: Symbolizes a select order of liberalist who have built a religion (mystic worship) on the "Mysteries of the Scriptures". They are in control of the eye of Lucifer and believe they are chosen of Satan to control the finances of the world.
Swasitika or Sun Wheel: An ancient religious symbol used long before Hitler came upon the scene. In Sun God worship, it is supposed to represent the sun's course in the heavens. Goathead: The horned god, Goat of Mendes, Baphomet, God of the Witches, the Scapegoat. It is a Satanist's way of mocking Christ as the "Lamb" who died for the sins of humanity.
Upside Down Cross or Southern Cross: Symbolizes mockery and rejection of the cross of Christ.

Broken Cross or Peace Sign:Again it is a mockery of the Cross of Christ. A person joining Satanism may, in a ritual, be handed a ceramic cross to invert and break down the cross bars in order to show rejection of Jesus.
Yin-Yang: In Chinese philosophy, two great opposite principles or forces on whose interplay everything in the universe depends. Satanic "S" or Broken "S": Represents a thunderbolt and means "Destroyer". In mythology it was the weapon of Zeus. This medallion is worn to give power over others. This symbol was worn by the members of the feared SS of Nazi Germany.
Star and Cresent: Here, the moon goddess "Dianna" and the morning star of "Lucifer" are represented. Satanic Cross: An upside down question mark in the cross of Christ, questioning the deity of God.

Scarab Beetle: This is the dung beetle which is the ancient Egyptian symbol of reincarnation.

 Udjat or All-Seeing Eye: One of the few symbols referring to Lucifer (the King of Hell). The one whom, it is thought, will pass judgement.
Horned Hand: The "Horned Hand" is the sign of recognition between those who are in the occult. Anarchy: The symbol of "Anarchy" represents the abolition of ALL law. Initially, those in "Punk" music used this symbol, but it is now widely used by heavy metal followers.
Signs of the Zodiac: Are used in the Satanic or the occult worship of the gods and goddesses that seek after the lust of Ishtar. Conjuring the spirits of fertility to produce every type of immoral lust after and for the gods of procreation is done through these emblems.
